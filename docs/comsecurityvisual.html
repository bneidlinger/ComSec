AI-Enhanced Seismic Sheathing

Securing Communication Lines Against Physical Tampering & Sabotage

Concept Visualization

Continuous seismic vibration monitoring, with AI anomaly detection, layered around sensitive communication lines.

Key Features

Inline Seismic Sensing
Miniaturized sensors embedded along the cable sheath monitor for vibration, flex, cut, or drilling attempts.
AI-Based Learning
Onboard edge AI learns the normal vibration patterns of the building, differentiating between harmless background noise and true tampering.
Active Tamper Alarm
When a significant anomaly is detected, the system triggers an immediate alarm or notifies a centralized panel.
Physical Reinforcement
Sheath material is highly tear, cut, and drill-resistant, making physical bypass extremely difficult.
Works with Any Line
Can be wrapped around RS-485, Ethernet, or even power lines for critical systems, supporting retrofits.

How It Works: Step-by-Step

1. Continuous Monitoring
Sensors pick up vibration data along the length of the protected line.
2. Edge AI Analysis
AI/ML models run on embedded chips, constantly analyzing for deviations from learned “normal” activity.
3. Threat Detection
Unusual activity—such as wire cutting, heavy vibration, or attempts at splicing—triggers the system.
4. Alarm & Alert
Alerts are sent to the security panel and/or SOC, and optionally local sounder/strobe activation or lockdown.
5. Response & Forensics
System logs are reviewed; AI improves with feedback from incident response and real-world events.

Technical Potential & Applications

  • Critical infrastructure (government, utilities, data centers)
  • High-security commercial and industrial facilities
  • ATM, bank, or server room conduits
  • Military and defense comms
  • Retrofit for legacy alarm panels
